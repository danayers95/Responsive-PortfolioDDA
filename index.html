About Me

I'm a 25 year old drummer, writer, dog-sitter, and server, but music has always been the main thing that has defined me. Playing music has given me the opportunity to play my music all over the east coast, as well as talk to fans in different places all over the world. I've helped create seven full-length studio albums, four EPs, and six music videos.
